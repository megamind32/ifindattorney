## What Was Built

A completely new multi-step form page that enables users across all of Nigeria to find legal services. The old single-page Lagos-only form has been replaced with a modern, fluid 4-step progressive form.

### 4-Step Form Flow

1. **Step 1: Legal Need**
   - Select from 8 practice areas (checkboxes) OR describe issue (textarea)
   - These are mutually exclusive for cleaner data

2. **Step 2: Location (Nigeria-Wide)**
   - Select state from dropdown (36 + FCT = 37 options)
   - Dynamic zone selector updates based on state (4-13 zones per state)
   - Examples: Lagos has 13 zones, Abuja has 8, Kano has 6, etc.

3. **Step 3: Budget**
   - Choose from 6 Nigerian Naira ranges
   - ₦50k → ₦100k → ₦250k → ₦500k → ₦1M → Above ₦1M

4. **Step 4: Review & Submit**
   - Summary of all selections
   - Privacy notice
   - Submit saves to sessionStorage and navigates to results

## How It Works

### User Journey
```
1. User navigates to /form
2. Sees Step 1 (Legal Need)
   - Selects employment law + corporate law
   - Progress shows 25%
3. Clicks Next → Goes to Step 2
4. Selects "Lagos" state
   - Zone dropdown appears with 13 Lagos zones
   - Selects "Victoria Island"
   - Progress shows 50%
5. Clicks Next → Goes to Step 3
6. Selects "₦250,000 - ₦500,000" budget
   - Progress shows 75%
7. Clicks Next → Goes to Step 4 (Review)
8. Sees summary of selections
   - Legal Need: Employment Law, Corporate Law
   - Location: Lagos, Victoria Island
   - Budget: ₦250,000 - ₦500,000
9. Clicks "Find My Lawyers"
   - Saves to sessionStorage
   - Navigates to /results
10. Results page loads matching lawyers
```
